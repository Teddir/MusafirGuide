Why the Difference?

Differences in determining travel distance (Qasar) arise from interpreting evidence from the Prophet's (PBUH) Hadith. Some scholars rely on distance (marhalah), while others rely on custom ('urf) or travel time. This app uses the famous (mu'tamad) opinion from each school to simplify it for modern travelers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is distance calculated?

We use modern routing technology (Leaflet Routing Machine / OSRM) to calculate real travel distance based on roads, not just a straight line. This provides results more accurate to your actual journey.

Is Qasar allowed if staying for 3 days?

The majority of schools allow Qasar if the intention is to stay less than 4 days. However, this depends on the detailed conditions and your intention. Please check the comparison table above.
